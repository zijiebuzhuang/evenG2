🎙️ VoiceInk — Let voice flow like ink.

Just built a Chinese speech-to-text app for the G2! Think of it as a pocket transcriber that lives on your glasses.

Here's what it does:
- Real-time Chinese transcription powered by iFlytek ASR
- Smart silence detection — it listens for natural pauses in your speech and auto-transcribes (no button mashing needed)
- Three-state recording: start → pause → resume → stop, nice and smooth
- Every transcript comes with timestamps so you know exactly when things were said
- Recording history saved locally — swipe left to delete, tap to review full transcripts
- G2 display shows live status and the latest transcript right on your glasses

The backend runs a lightweight Node.js server that does the heavy lifting. Audio comes in as raw PCM, the server monitors volume levels, and when you stop talking for ~1.5 seconds it fires off the recognition. There's also a 10-second safety net so long monologues don't get lost.

Built with Vite + vanilla JS, no frameworks, keeps things fast and simple. Groq Whisper tab is there too for English transcription.
