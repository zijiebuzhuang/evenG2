MoonWalker v1.3.0 - Walk mindfully with zen quotes on your G2

MoonWalker is a minimal AR walking navigation app. Instead of staring at your phone, you get a simple direction arrow and distance on your G2 glasses - just enough to guide you, so you can stay present in the moment.

While you walk, philosophy quotes from Stoicism, Zen Buddhism, and Taoism appear gently on the display.

What's in v1.3.0:
- Dark mode support
- Improved SDK lifecycle (clean startup/shutdown)
- Navigation works without glasses connected (for testing)
- Walk simulator tool for development

Features:
- Minimal navigation: direction arrow + distance on G2
- Zen & philosophy quotes during walks (ZenQuotes API)
- Dual map support: Amap (China) + OpenStreetMap (global)
- Search by name or address
- Double-click glasses to toggle navigation
- Navigation history for quick access
- Configurable quote sources and display settings
